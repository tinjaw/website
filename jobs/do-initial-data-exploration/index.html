      Do Initial Data Exploration

      Quickly understand the content and quality of a dataset.

      When searching for datasets or just receiving one from a third party, you need quick ways of checking what it contains and evaluating the quality in general.

      Context

      It's part of the task of starting to work with a new dataset.

      Initially, you may do this with a Unix tool such as sed or open in Excel. Over time, you will want to have descriptive statistics and attribute descriptions directly in the data hub and in the terminal.

      # Examples

      wc mydata.csv
grep "Rome" mydata.csv | wc
xsv mydata.csv

      # Solutions

      DataHub
      It already provides simple visualizations for every dataset, right from the web.

      Data Curator
      Quickly analyze datasets in a simple desktop application.
